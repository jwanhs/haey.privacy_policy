Common Questions

The dictionary does not find a word. What should I do?

Check the spelling, try the lemma or base form of the word, and try switching the lookup direction. Kurdish words may appear under a canonical form rather than every inflected form.

Does the app work offline?

Yes. The App is designed around bundled offline dictionary data and local caching. Some optional pronunciation or media-related behavior may require an internet connection.

Why does speech input ask for microphone permission?

The microphone is used only when you choose voice input. Speech recognition is handled by your device or operating system. You can deny or revoke microphone access in your device settings.

How do I clear local data?

Use the app settings where available, or clear the app's data from your device settings. Uninstalling the app may also remove app-managed local data.

How can I report a wrong translation or missing entry?

Email master@haey.org with the searched word, source language, target language, expected result, and the result you saw in the app. The more exact the report, the faster it can be checked.

About Kurdish Dictionary Glosses

Kurdish Dictionary Glosses is a Kurdish-first dictionary app backed by offline dictionary data and Wiktionary-derived language resources. It is built to help users search, compare, and share Kurdish glossary entries across supported languages.
